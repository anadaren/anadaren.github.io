About

Hi, call me Ana!

I'm a 24 year old game developer, extended reality designer, and 3D artist! I am currently based in Brooklyn, New York, and love designing projects that sit at the intersecion of the creative and the technical.

I have recently completed my MS in Integrated Design & Media, at NYU Tandon. Before that, I graduated from TCNJ, where I majored in Interactive Multimedia with a minor in Women, Gender, & Sexuality Studies. I enjoy programming and creating art, and have been playing, studying, and creating games for years!

I loves solving puzzles, and I'm passionate about designing games, other interactive experiences, and bringing digital worlds to life!

I'm always open to work on new projects and improve my creative skills!
